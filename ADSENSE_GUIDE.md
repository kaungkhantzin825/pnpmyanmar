# AdSense Integration Guide

## Why This Implementation is Safe

This video blog platform is designed with AdSense best practices to **avoid invalid traffic** and policy violations.

## Built-in Safety Features

### 1. Strategic Ad Placement
- Ads are placed in natural, non-intrusive positions
- No ads near clickable elements (buttons, links)
- Proper spacing between ads and content
- Maximum 3 ads per page recommended

### 2. Ad Position System
The platform offers 6 controlled positions:

#### ‚úÖ RECOMMENDED (Safe & Effective)
- **Sidebar**: Always visible, non-intrusive
- **Content Middle**: After video, natural viewing flow
- **Footer**: End of page, expected location

#### ‚ö†Ô∏è USE CAREFULLY

### 3. Invalid Traffic Prevention

#### What We Do:
- No misleading ad placements
- No ads disguised as content
- No encouragement to click ads
- Proper ad labeling (handled by AdSense)
- Natural user flow maintained

#### What You Should Do:
- Monitor your traffic sources
- Avoid bot traffic
- Don't click your own ads
- Don't ask others to click
- Report suspicious activity

## Setting Up AdSense

### Step 1: Get Approved

1. **Apply for AdSense**
   - Visit: https://www.google.com/adsense
   - Sign up with your Google account
   - Add your website URL
   - Wait for approval (1-2 weeks)

2. **Requirements for Approval**
   - Original, quality content
   - At least 10-15 posts
   - Regular posting schedule
   - Privacy policy page
   - About page
   - Contact information
   - Clean, professional design

### Step 2: Create Ad Units

1. **Login to AdSense**
2. **Go to Ads ‚Üí By ad unit**
3. **Create Display Ads**

#### Recommended Ad Sizes:

**For Sidebar:**
- 300x250 (Medium Rectangle) - Best
- 300x600 (Half Page) - Good for long pages
- 160x600 (Wide Skyscraper) - Alternative

**For Content Areas:**
- 728x90 (Leaderboard) - Desktop
- 336x280 (Large Rectangle) - High performance
- 320x100 (Mobile Banner) - Mobile devices

**For Footer:**
- 728x90 (Leaderboard) - Standard
- 970x90 (Large Leaderboard) - Wide screens

### Step 3: Add Ads to Your Site

1. **Copy Ad Code**
   - In AdSense, click "Get code"
   - Copy the entire code block

2. **Add to Admin Panel**
   - Login to `/admin/dashboard`
   - Go to "AdSense Ads"
   - Click "Add New Ad"
   - Paste your code
   - Select position
   - Save

3. **Verify Display**
   - Visit your blog
   - Check if ads appear
   - Test on mobile devices

## Warning Signs

### Watch Out For:
- Sudden traffic spikes
- Unusually high CTR (>5%)
- Traffic from suspicious sources
- Multiple clicks from same IP
- Bot-like behavior patterns

### If You See These:
1. Check your traffic sources
2. Review recent changes
3. Contact AdSense support
4. Temporarily disable suspicious ads
5. Document everything

## Troubleshooting

### Ads Not Showing?

**Check:**
1. Ad code is correct
2. Ad is set to "Active"
3. Position is correct
4. No ad blockers active
5. AdSense account is approved

**Wait Time:**
- New ads: 10-20 minutes
- New sites: 24-48 hours
- After changes: 1-2 hours

### Low Earnings?

**Improve:**
1. Increase traffic quality
2. Optimize ad positions
3. Create better content
4. Improve user engagement
5. Test different ad sizes

### Invalid Traffic Warning?

**Actions:**
1. Review traffic sources
2. Check for bot traffic
3. Remove suspicious sources
4. Document your findings
